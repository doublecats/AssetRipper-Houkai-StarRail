* Scenes
* Prefabs (GameObjects with transform components)
* AnimationClips (legacy, generic, humanoid)
* Meshes (various formats)
* Shaders (dummy)
* Textures
* Audio
* Fonts
* Movie textures
* Materials
* AnimatorControllers
* Avatars
* Terrains
* TextAssets
* Components:
  * MeshRenderer
  * SkinnedMeshRenderer
  * Animation
  * Animator
  * Canvas
  * Light
  * ParticleSystem
  * Colliders
  * Rigidbody
  * AudioSource
  * Camera
  * MonoBehaviour (Mono and Il2Cpp)
  * MonoScript (Mono and Il2Cpp)
